What is this?

The Office Space Analysis template is a way to determine immediate and future space requirements.

Instructions

  1. Enter the load factor percentage *
  2. Enter the circulation percentage *
  3. Enter the Length and Width of each space category
  4. Enter the quantity of each space category
  5. Enter the total number of people who will work in each space category
  6. Repeat, if needed, for future space required

Download

Notes

  1. Load factor percentage is the portion of the building dedicated to common areas. This percentage common areas. When added to the Usable Square Footage (USF) it becomes Rentable Square Footage (RSF). For additional and detailed information, please visit BOMA .
  2. Circulation percentage is way to account for the space used to move around in. Closed plans which utilize more private offices typically have smaller circulation requirements (20-30%). Open and hybrid plans may be higher (30-50%) depending on the space requirements. The circulation percentage, or factor, provides the circulation multiplier which is used to determine the additional space. It will defer from the graph circulation percentage due to the RSF applied from the load factor.
